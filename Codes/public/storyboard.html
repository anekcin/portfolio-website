Intent of Web Pages

  • Home: An introduction to who I am, my interests, and what visitors can expect from the site.

  • Professional: Showcases my skills, projects, experience, and career aspirations—ideal for networking and job opportunities.

  • Personal Development: Focused on my growth, goals, and learning journey, possibly including achievements and reflections.

  • Personality: Gives insight into who I am beyond my professional side, highlighting my hobbies, values, and interests.

  • Storyboard: A sturctured blueprint for the website showing how everything came together.

  • Reference: A collection of resources, recommendations, or links relevant to my work, studies, or personal development.

Theme

The colour choices for the website were intended to depict professionality, calmness and trustworthiness.

  • Dark Blue (#27445D): Represents reliability, intelligence, and depth. It gives the site a strong and stable feel, aligning with your background in Computer Science and software development.

  • Teal Green (#497D74): Symbolizes balance, growth, and innovation. This hints at creativity, adaptability, and a forward-thinking approach—great qualities for someone passionate about problem-solving and technology.

  • White (#FFFFFF): Stands for simplicity, clarity, and openness. It ensures readability and a clean design, emphasizing attention to detail and efficiency.
