Hi, I'm Abigail Nickena Robinson, a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Technology with a passion for software development. I enjoy solving problems through code and continuously expanding my skills as I work on projects throughout my degree. My goal is to become a software engineer, creating innovative solutions that make a real impact.

Completing a crochet piece brings a sense of accomplishment and joy that mirrors the feeling of building functional software. I also have a keen interest in photography, capturing moments through a unique lens, and I find solace in reading fictional novels and poetry.

At my core, I believe in being a good person, staying curious, and striving for growth in everything I do. Whether through technology or creative expression, I aim to make a positive difference
